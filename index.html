From fake moisturizers to repackaged lipsticks, counterfeit cosmetics are flooding global markets. For premium beauty brands, one counterfeit product can destroy customer trust and trigger a PR crisis.

High demand, influencer-driven trends, and unregulated e-commerce make it easy for counterfeiters to thrive. Once your product leaves the warehouse, how can you guarantee it’s still yours?
